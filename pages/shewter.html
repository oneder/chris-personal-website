ShEWtER

Point-and-Click shooter prototype made with Flash and AS3 for the Computer Game Design program at Manchester Community College.
Objective: Kill sewer creeps as long as you can
Controls: Left Click - Shoot, Mouse - Aim (Circle bombs with cursor to pick them up)
